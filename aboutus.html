    TARGET AUDIENCE

    People who are willing to donate blood and save lives are the main target market for the fast blood donor finder website.

    This includes anybody who satisfies the requirements for blood donation, regardless of their age, gender, or origin.

    Regular blood donors looking for a convenient blood donation site.

    First-time blood donors looking to discover an appropriate site and learn more about the donation process.

    Blood donors in need of an immediate blood donation who need to locate a blood bank or hospital in the area.

    Hospitals and blood banks

    Additionally, the website targets blood banks and medical facilities that need a consistent supply of blood for transfusions. The technology enables these organizations to more effectively manage their blood supply and connect with potential donors.
